What is Y2K?

Y2K refers to the period from the late 1990s to the early 2000s. It was a transitional era marked by rapid change, much like the shift from analog to digital. During this time, technology, culture, and everyday life were undergoing significant transformations.

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to capture and preserve images of past winters in Korea. By collecting people's personal memories and moments, I aim to show that even as time passes, memories continue to exist somewhere, in one form or another.
